About Project:

 This project is designed to provide an introduction to Laboratory Information Management Systems (LIMS).
This will include different features and functions that allow users to effectively manage samples and their associated data.
After all, you will have a deeper appreciation of the processes behind the powerhouse that make a laboratory very successful.

Who can use this project:
1-shopkeepers
2-Medical Technologists
3-Students of Laboratory Science
4-Lab Professionals
